### 1. Check CPU Usage
- `top`: Real-time process monitoring.
- `htop`: Graphical process viewer.

### 2. Process Discovery
- `ps aux`: List all running processes.
- `ps aux | grep ssh`: Find specific process (e.g., SSH).

### 3. Killing Processes
- `kill <PID>`: Graceful kill.
- `kill -9 <PID>`: Force kill (SigKill).
